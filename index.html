01 / About

I'm a data and backend engineer who spent years in uniform before moving into software — that background shows up in how I build: methodical, accountable for the outcome, and allergic to systems nobody can explain under pressure.

Most of my work sits at the intersection of finance and engineering: reconciling contract terms against real transactions, giving executives proof-first answers instead of black-box dashboards, and replacing spreadsheet-run operations with systems that don't fall over. I work comfortably across the stack — Snowflake and SQL on the data side, FastAPI and Django on the backend, React and Streamlit on the front end — and increasingly, I design agentic AI pipelines that reason over that data rather than just display it.

I'm based in Florida and open to senior data engineering, backend engineering, or AI/automation engineering roles.
