Project 06 / Production operations software

Meal Prep Ops

A production Django app that runs the day-to-day operations of a real meal-prep business — clients, meal plans, inventory, and kitchen production.

Live in production
Django 5.2 HTMX Alpine.js Tailwind Postgres

01 Problem

A growing meal-prep business ran its client meal plans, ingredient inventory, and weekly kitchen production entirely on spreadsheets. There was no single source of truth across the business, and nothing mobile-friendly for kitchen staff working on their feet during production days.

02 Solution

A full Django application with an owner-facing client and meal-plan editor, a weekly production planner that aggregates every client's individual plan into raw-pound thaw tasks and packing lists, a mobile-first kitchen view — thaw sheets, packing cards — installable as a PWA on staff phones, and an inventory dashboard with automatic shortfall alerts.

03 Architecture

A Django 5.2 app (HTMX + Alpine.js + Tailwind on the front end) backs onto Postgres, is fully Dockerized, and deploys to Railway with Sentry error tracking in production. The owner portal, kitchen mobile views, and inventory dashboard are separate modules that share one underlying data model.

Fig. 1 — Three modules share one Postgres model; the whole app is Dockerized and deployed on Railway with Sentry monitoring.

04 Tech stack

Django 5.2 HTMX Alpine.js Tailwind Postgres Docker Railway Sentry

05 Highlights

  • Atomic per-week production planning with per-ingredient shrinkage-factor math built in.
  • Color-coded inventory alerts (red / yellow / green) driven by 7-day rolling demand.
  • Installable PWA for kitchen staff — thaw sheets and packing cards work on any phone.
  • Public, read-only client share links for meal plans without exposing the admin app.

06 Status

Live in production on Railway, running as an internal business tool. The application, source repository, and operational data are kept private to protect client information.
